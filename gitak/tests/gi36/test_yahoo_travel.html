Simple matrix sample with mock data, testing the data showing and query etc.
Test yahoo_travel
include testSetup.html  
jsxopen ${GIPATH}/shell.html?jsxapppath=${SAMPLESPATH}/yahoo_travel  
waitForJsxButtonPresent Get Travel Info 5000
click gi=button/[@jsxtext='Get Travel Info']  
waitForElementPresent JsxMatrixCellText=matrix1,Canada  
typeJsxTextbox gi=query France
click JsxButtonText=Query by name  
assertElementPresent JsxMatrixCellText=matrix1,France  
verifyElementNotPresent JsxMatrixCellText=matrix1,China  
click gi=button/[@jsxtext='Get Travel Info']  
typeJsxTextbox gi=image img/Icon.jpg
typeJsxTextbox gi=name Singapore
typeJsxTextbox gi=link http://www.generalinterface.org/
typeJsxTextbox gi=text Lucy
typeJsxTextbox gi=date Oct 29,2013
click JsxButtonText=Insert  
assertElementPresent JsxMatrixCellText=matrix1,Singapore
